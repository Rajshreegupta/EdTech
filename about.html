Who are we?

Learners' Point is an initiative to break all barriers faced in gaining knowledge. No Barrier of College , No Barrier of Stream, No Barrier of Age, No Barrier of Genders. Learn what you want to. All you need is a zeal to explore. Learners' Point is non-profitable organization. The amount collected shall be utilized in providing the gift of knowledge to unpriviledged. We are working in collaboraton with Amazon, the world's leading Tech gaint.
